Our Services

Empowering Farmers with Climate-Smart Solutions

Adag-Anii Company Limited supports farmers across 19 districts in Northern Uganda with climate-smart services, from farmer group development to market linkages, ensuring sustainable livelihoods.

Cocoa Production

We cultivate Forastero and Trinitario cocoa across 492 acres with 842 farmers, using sustainable practices and nursery support in Lira, Oyam, Nwoya, and Apac.

Learn More
Farmer Group Development

We mobilize farmers through churches, cultural, and political leaders to form strong cooperatives, supporting 1,500 farmers in rice and 842 in cocoa.

Learn More
Organic Farm Inputs

Our eco-friendly inputs from nurseries in Lira, Oyam, Nwoya, and Apac enhance soil health for organic cocoa, vegetables, and rice production.

Learn More
Post-Harvest Management

We train farmers in harvesting, fermentation, and drying, and manage post-harvest processes to ensure high-quality cocoa for global markets.

Learn More
Market Linkages

Partnering with ICAM Chocolates, we connect farmers to global markets, ensuring fair prices and eliminating middlemen for sustainable incomes.
