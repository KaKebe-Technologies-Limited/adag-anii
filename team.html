Our Governance

Our Organizational Structure

Adag-Anii Limited’s governance framework ensures sustainable growth and accountability, guiding our mission in cocoa, vegetable, rice production, and more.

Our Organogram

Our structure places the Board of Directors at the helm, overseeing strategic direction. The Management Team, led by the CEO, drives operations across cocoa production, vegetable farming, rice cultivation, organic inputs, and market linkages, supported by dedicated departments for finance, operations, and community engagement.

Board of Directors

Our experienced board members provide strategic oversight, ensuring Adag-Anii Limited’s commitment to sustainability and excellence.

Okullo Peter Paul

Managing Director

Okullo Peter Paul is a holder of a BSc Agribusiness and a Certificate in Agribusiness Management with 15 years of experience in multiplication and development of the rice value chain. He also has 10 years of experience in organic vegetable growing and 11 years experience in cocoa production with expertise in best agronomic practices for great yields in northern Uganda.

Obuku David

Director Human Resources

With a Bachelors of Education and 19 years of experience in the field as well as 8 years of experience in managing human resources, great experience in human capital development in agricultural production, Obuku David drives innovation in our cocoa and rice production strategies.

Martin Ogwang

Director Operation

A holder of a Masters in Development Studies, Degree in Social Sciences, and a Diploma in Education, Project Planning and Development Martin Ogwang has experience of 35 years in community development, livelihood project management, and organizational management.
